About Me

Clergy & Provost of the Cathedral

Very Rev. Dr. Isaac Adeyemi Adeniji was born on 6th January, 1964 at Orile-ilugun in Odeda Local Government of Ogun State. My father was Late Mr. Isaiah Aremu Oluwabunmi Adeniji a Pharmacist Technician (then called Dispenser), He was the parish council secretary of our Church for over 20years. My mother is Chief Mrs. Felicia Olabisi Adeniji – The Iya Egba Aya Bishop of St. Mary Church, Osiele my Martian village Church in Odeda Local Government. I grew up early in life to serve the church as altar server, choir and a cross bearer. I gave my life to Jesus in June 1982 at Deeper Life Conference held in Ibadan and continue to grow steadily in the faith. On Friday 23rd September, 1983 through the ministration of our referred Father, The Rt. Rev. Dr. Emmanuel Bolanle Gbonigi Bishop of Akure Diocese, I rechecked my faith again, hence I join the church ministry.
